隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

《噪音分贝测试仪》（以下简称“本应用”）非常重视用户的隐私与数据安全。本隐私政策将说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。使用本应用即表示您同意本隐私政策。

1. 我们收集和使用的信息 / Information We Collect and Use

麦克风权限 / Microphone: 本应用使用设备麦克风来测量环境噪音分贝。数据仅在本地处理，不会录制、存储或上传到服务器。
The app uses your device microphone to measure environmental noise levels. Data is processed locally and is not recorded, stored, or uploaded.

相机权限 / Camera: 拍照功能需要调用设备相机，用于生成带分贝水印的照片。照片仅保存在设备本地，不会上传或分享。
The app uses your device camera for taking photos with decibel watermark. Photos are stored locally and are not uploaded or shared.

存储权限 / Storage: 用于保存生成的带分贝水印的照片。所有数据保存在设备本地，不会上传或共享。
Storage access is used to save photos with decibel watermark. All data is stored locally and not shared.

2. 信息的使用方式 / How We Use Information

收集的权限仅用于提供核心功能：测量噪音、拍照并生成水印图片。不会用于其他目的。
Permissions are only used for core functions: measuring noise, taking photos, and adding watermarks. Data is not used for other purposes.

3. 信息的共享与披露 / Sharing and Disclosure

本应用不会将任何个人信息、照片或麦克风数据上传、存储或分享给第三方。
The app does not upload, store, or share any personal information, photos, or microphone data with third parties.

如果未来接入广告服务（如 Google AdMob），广告可能会使用设备信息（如广告 ID）来提供个性化广告，但不会涉及您的声音或照片内容。
If advertising services (e.g., Google AdMob) are used in the future, ads may use device information (like Advertising ID) for personalized ads, without accessing your audio or photos.

4. 数据安全 / Data Security

本应用不会在服务器端存储任何数据。您的照片和测量结果均保存在设备本地，由您自行管理。
The app does not store any data on servers. Your photos and measurement results are stored locally and managed by you.

5. 儿童隐私 / Children's Privacy

本应用不面向13岁以下儿童。若您是儿童用户，请在监护人指导下使用本应用。
The app is not intended for children under 13. Children should use the app under parental guidance.

6. 本隐私政策的更新 / Updates to This Policy

我们可能根据功能调整或法律法规变化更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策随应用版本发布，请及时查看。
We may update this policy based on feature changes or legal requirements. Updated policy will be released with app versions; please check regularly.
